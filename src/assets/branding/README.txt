Place your branding images here to auto-populate the Branding gallery.

Supported formats: .png, .jpg, .jpeg, .webp

File names become project titles (underscores/dashes are converted to spaces and capitalized).

Example:
  working-on-living-campaign-01.jpg
  logo_mark_concept.png

These will appear when you click the Branding card in the Graphic Design section.
